Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HexBuzz ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application, web application, and desktop application (collectively, the "App").

Key Points:
  • We only collect data necessary to provide our services
  • We use Google Sign-In for authentication
  • Your game scores and progress are stored in the cloud
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address: Your Google account email
  • Display name: Your Google profile name
  • Profile photo: Your Google profile picture URL
  • User ID: A unique identifier from Google

1.2 Game Data

We collect information about your gameplay:

  • Game progress: Levels completed, stars earned
  • Leaderboard scores: Your total stars and rank
  • Daily challenge results: Completion time and stars
  • Timestamps: When you play levels and complete challenges

1.3 Device Information

For push notifications, we may collect:

  • Device token: A unique identifier for sending notifications
  • Platform: Android, iOS, Web, or Windows
  • Notification preferences: Which notifications you've enabled

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • App usage: Features you use, time spent in app
  • Performance data: Crash reports, app performance metrics
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

1.5 Guest Mode

If you play without signing in:

  • We only store game progress locally on your device
  • No personal information is collected or stored on our servers
  • Leaderboard and daily challenge features are not available

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

2.1 Core Services

  • Authenticating your identity and maintaining your account
  • Storing and syncing your game progress across devices
  • Displaying leaderboards and rankings
  • Managing daily challenges and related leaderboards

2.2 Communication

  • Sending push notifications about daily challenges (if enabled)
  • Notifying you when your leaderboard rank changes significantly (if enabled)
  • Re-engagement notifications if you haven't played recently (if enabled)

2.3 Analytics and Improvement

  • Understanding how users interact with the App
  • Identifying and fixing bugs and performance issues
  • Improving game design and user experience
  • Generating aggregated, anonymized statistics

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Complying with legal obligations and court orders
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored using Google Cloud Platform services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Account credentials
  • Cloud Firestore: Game progress, leaderboards, daily challenges
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Device tokens for notifications

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Data encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Data encrypted at rest by Google Cloud Platform
  • Firestore security rules to prevent unauthorized access
  • Authentication required for all personal data access
  • Regular security audits and updates

3.3 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: After 3 years of inactivity, we may delete your account
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Public Information

The following information is visible to all App users:

  • Your display name on leaderboards
  • Your profile photo on leaderboards
  • Your total stars and rank
  • Your daily challenge completion times and rankings

4.3 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the App:

  • Google/Firebase: Authentication, database, notifications, analytics
  • Advertising partners: For serving ads (if applicable)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government requests for national security purposes
  • Protecting our rights, property, or safety
  • Preventing fraud or illegal activities

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke consent for data processing

5.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

5.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  • In-app: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Email: Contact us at privacy@hexbuzz.app (update with your contact)
  • Response time: We will respond within 30 days

5.4 Data Portability

You can export your data in JSON format:

  • Go to Settings > Account > Export My Data
  • You will receive a downloadable file with all your game data

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for children, but we take special precautions:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Google Sign-In requires users to be at least 13 years old (or older in some countries)
  • Children can play in Guest Mode without creating an account
  • If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13 without consent, we will delete it promptly

Parents can contact us to review, delete, or stop the collection of their child's information.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, where Google Cloud Platform operates data centers.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Google Cloud Platform complies with GDPR and other privacy frameworks
  • Data transfers comply with Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data
